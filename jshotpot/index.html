“Healthy Eating, Healthy Life!!”

Hotpot, a stew of variety of fresh ingredients cook in a metal pot, has captured the essence of home-style Asian cooking in a charming and refreshingly comfortable way. In Asian culture, hot pot is not just food, but also contain a lot of connotation that show how family should be unite with each other by gathering around the metal pot. It is one of the unique historic cuisine that has passed down from generation to generation.

Mini Hotpot was founded by Jay Xue in Chamblee, GA in 2004. It was the first mini hotpot restaurant in the Atlanta area until the second location opened in Duluth in 2009. Mini Hotpot follows strict standard, outstanding quality, and vivid presentation to deliver exceptional experience for all customers. All our ingredients are locally sourced where possible for us to provide the freshest and healthiest to our customers.

We, at Mini Hotpot, serve one pot per guest, each pot is personal-sized to ensure complete satisfaction and cleanliness. We offer the highest quality ingredients with choice of 7 freshly-made broths. On the side, we also have our own unique house sauces for each individuals to mix with his or her preference. Healthy dining has never been so satisfying.

Mini Hotpot offers authentic Chinese cuisine for many years in the greater Atlanta area and will continue to explore and serve the best to our customers! Mini Hotpot is recently renamed to J’s Mini Hotpot!! Come and enjoy a fun yet exceptional experience!
